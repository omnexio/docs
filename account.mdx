## 🔑 Login & Security

- Secure login with email or social accounts
- Optional **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for added protection

## 💬 Comprehensive Chat History

- All your conversations are stored securely
- Powered by **Elasticsearch** for lightning-fast search and retrieval
- Revisit past answers, sources, and insights anytime

## ⚙️ Account Settings

- **UI Language** – switch interface language instantly
- **Dark Theme** – toggle for comfortable viewing
- **Message Fonts** – adjust chat font style to your preference
- **Audio Features** – play responses as audio or **dictate messages by voice**

## 📂 File Management

- Upload and securely store your own files
- Supported formats: PDFs, Excel, text files (DOC files not yet supported)
- Files can be analyzed with any of our **AI agents** or **models**

## 🤖 Flexible Agents & Models

- Switch between agents and models on the fly
- Use **@agent-name** or **@model-name** in chat with autocomplete suggestions
- Example: `@AIWriter draft an email` or `@ClaudeSonnet4 summarize this report`

## 🧩 Free vs Premium

- **Free tier** → access to core search and basic agents
- **Premium plans** → unlock advanced agents, file storage, history search, and API access
