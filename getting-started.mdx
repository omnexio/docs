## 🔍 Quick Start

1. Go to the [Omnexio.ai homepage](https://omnexio.ai).
2. Type your question in natural language.
3. Get **instant answers with citations** pulled from live sources.

You don’t need an account to try Omnexio.ai.
Just ask your first question on the homepage and explore the results.


## 👤 Create an Account

If you want to personalize your experience:

- Save searches for later
- Access premium AI templates
- Manage preferences and history

Click the **Sign Up** button on the homepage to register.

Omnexio.ai offers a **free tier** for casual users and **premium plans** for advanced features and API access.
